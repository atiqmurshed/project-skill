Animals

1. Lion

The lion is known as the king of the jungle. It is a large carnivorous mammal found in Africa and parts of Asia.

2. Elephant

The elephant is the largest land animal in the world. They are herbivores and are known for their intelligence and memory.

3. Tiger

The tiger is a powerful carnivorous animal known for its beautiful striped fur. Tigers are mainly found in Asia.
